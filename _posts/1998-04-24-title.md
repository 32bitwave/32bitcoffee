Programming
- C, C#, Python

Electronics & Microcontrollers
- Basic Circuit design
- Arduino and Raspberry Pi projects

Digital Fabrication
- 3D printing and modeling
- Rapid prototyping

Each project includes instructions, minimal materials, and troubleshooting tips.
